FAQs

How do I choose the right design professional for my project?

Start by browsing through the profiles and portfolios of various professionals. Look for those who have experience in projects similar to yours and check their client reviews. You can also schedule a consultation to discuss your project in detail and assess their compatibility with your vision.

What should I include in my project brief when seeking a design professional?

Your project brief should include details such as the scope of the project, your budget, preferred style or theme, any specific requirements or constraints, and a timeline. Providing clear and detailed information helps professionals understand your needs and provide accurate proposals.

How much does it cost to hire a design professional for a project?

Design professionals set their own rates, so the cost will vary depending on the project scope, experience level, and location.

How do I get started?

Simply create a free account on our platform. You can then browse design professionals, post your project, or contact individual profiles based on your needs.

What should I expect during the design process?

The design process usually involves initial consultations, concept development, design proposals, revisions based on your feedback, and finalization of the design. Once you approve the design, the professional may also assist with implementation or oversee the project’s execution.

What if I’m not satisfied with the final design?

If you’re not satisfied with the final design, first discuss your concerns with the professional to see if adjustments can be made. If the issue remains unresolved, our platform’s support team can assist in mediating the situation to find a satisfactory resolution.
